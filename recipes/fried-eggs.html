Fried Eggs

Description

This is a recipe of a delicious fried egg. Easy to make and a life-saver in moments when you are hungry and lazy.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Pour the oil on a frying pan and turn on the heat.
  2. Crack the egg onto the frying pan.
  3. Add the salt to the egg.
  4. Let it fry until the white part is hard.
