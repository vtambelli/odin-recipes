Pizza

Description

This is a recipe of a delicious pizza. Easy to make and suitable for all meals, after all there's nothing better than pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 10g yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Warm the water to body temperature.
  2. Add the yeast and wait until it foams.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour and the salt.
  4. Add the water to the flour mix and mix them together.
  5. Add the olive oil and mix it together until it forms a soft dough.
  6. Transfer the dough to a clean surface and knead it for about 10 minutes.
  7. Let it rest for about 1 hour or until it doubles the size.
  8. Shape the dough into a flat disc.
  9. Top it with whatever you wish.
  10. Bake for 20 minutes.
