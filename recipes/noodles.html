Noodles

Description

This is a recipe of delicious noodles. Junkies all-time favorite1

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Noodles
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. Pour the water into a pan and turn on the heat.
  2. When it starts boiling, add the noodles.
  3. Let it cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove 3/4 of the water.
  5. Add the seasoning powder and mix.
